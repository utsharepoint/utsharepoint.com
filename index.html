About us

A user group for you

We love the Microsoft 365 Apps. Each month we typically cover topics to help better understand these tools and encourage our members to showcase the success they are having in using these products.

  • Mission - Develop and Support a high-quality local community
  • Technologies - All things Microsoft 365
  • Events - Monthly User Groups and Annual SharePoint Saturday
